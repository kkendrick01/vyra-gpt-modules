The 3 Neurological Triggers

Your brain is wired to pay attention to these specific patterns. Most creators completely ignore them.

1

Pattern Interrupts

Something unexpected that breaks the normal pattern and forces the brain to pay attention. Your scroll-stopping secret weapon.

Example: Starting with a surprising statistic or counterintuitive statement
2

Emotional Contrast

Content that creates tension between current state and desired state. This gap drives engagement and shares.

Example: "While you're struggling with X, others are easily achieving Y"
3

Instant Gratification Promises

Clear indication that value will be delivered immediately. The brain craves quick rewards and clear payoffs.

Example: "In the next 60 seconds, you'll learn the exact framework..."
