The VyraCore Pyramid™

Hover each level to reveal what to write and why it works.

Level 1 — Pattern Interrupt

Create contrast with expectation. Present a fact or claim that forces a double-take. Keep it tight and verifiable.

Example shape: “In [year], [notable founder] did [counter-intuitive move] with [competitor].”

Level 2 — Emotional Hook

Surface the prevailing opinion or fear. Name the stakes people whisper about. The reader leans in when risk or payoff feels close.

Example shape: “Everyone called it reckless.”

Level 3 — Value Delivery

Reveal the mechanism or decision logic. Teach the hidden play. Use plain language and one clear causal chain.

Example shape: “The ‘charity’ maneuver accelerated adoption and boxed rivals.”

Level 4 — Engagement Trigger

Invite the next step with a reason to continue. Summon curiosity and participation with a breakdown, visual, or challenge.

Example shape: “Here’s why the rival never saw it coming.”

How to apply it today

Choose a counter-intuitive decision from your client’s domain. Write one sentence per level. Read it out loud. Remove filler. Publish when each line creates forward pull.

The VyraCore Pyramid™ — Field Guide

Levels

  • L1 — Pattern Interrupt: Counter-expectation fact. Short. Checkable.
  • L2 — Emotional Hook: Social judgment, pride, fear, or ambition.
  • L3 — Value Delivery: The mechanism. One clear causal chain.
  • L4 — Engagement Trigger: Teed-up next step with a visual or thread.

Prompts

  • What did the market misunderstand about this moment?
  • Which emotion drives attention here: fear, envy, hope?
  • What system or decision created the outcome?
  • What can the reader do or see next to embed the lesson?

Checklist

  • L1 is concrete and creates a double-take.
  • L2 raises stakes with a human angle.
  • L3 teaches one mechanism without jargon.
  • L4 opens a path to share, save, or continue.
