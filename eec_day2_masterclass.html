Strategic Authenticity — Field Guide

Use this sheet during client sessions and outline reviews.

Principles

  • Start with the audience’s curiosity. Name the tension.
  • Escalate consequences before offering relief.
  • Reveal a lived insight. Keep it grounded and useful.
  • Close with a concrete transformation or takeaway.

VyraPAST™ Prompts

  • P: What failure or friction does this audience feel weekly?
  • A: What breaks next if nothing changes? Who pays the price?
  • S: What action, system, or mindset shift did the client try?
  • T: What quantifiable outcome or repeatable lesson resulted?

Quality Checklist

  • First line raises a clear question in the reader’s mind.
  • Language is concrete; no filler or hedging.
  • Benefit is specific and observable.
  • CTA bridges to the next valuable step.
