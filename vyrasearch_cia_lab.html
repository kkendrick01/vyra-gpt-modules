How to Use Each Output

A. The Ideas

Each one represents a potential post, thread, or long-form content concept.
Choose the idea that best fits your niche or audience and move to expansion.

B. The Perplexity Link

Click "Expand this Idea" under any output. It opens a custom Perplexity search built from the Takeaway + Summary of that idea.

Use this page to:

  • • Verify data (names, dates, outcomes)
  • • Find credible sources or supporting quotes
  • • Gather deeper context before writing

C. Expansion Commands

Type directly into the chat:

Expand: [Title]

This tells the CIA to write a short factual mini-story with linked sources.

You can also run:

Regenerate 5 new ones but go deeper emotionally.

This raises emotional tension and variety.

Troubleshooting

Chat doesn't show "Please insert your inspiration hook"?
▼

The prompt wasn't pasted correctly. Start a new chat and paste the entire system block at once.

Ideas feel generic or off-topic?
▼

Your hook was too vague. Use specific, fact-based hooks with clear conflict or surprise elements.

Perplexity link doesn't match the idea?
▼

The encoding might be off. Copy the Takeaway + Summary manually and search Perplexity yourself.

Can I use this for client work?
▼

Yes! The CIA is designed for professional content operators. Always verify facts and adapt the voice to match your client's brand.
